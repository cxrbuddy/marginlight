# Marginlight Marketing

## 1. Product One-Liner

Your MacBook's daily wisdom island.

## 2. Positioning Statement

Marginlight is for readers, writers, students, founders, and creators who save meaningful quotes but rarely revisit them. It turns your favorite lines into one calm, beautiful quote per day, floating above your work without becoming another app to manage.

## 3. Founder Story

I save quotes from books all the time.

They end up in Markdown files, notes apps, highlights, screenshots, and forgotten folders.

The strange part is that the quotes are often exactly what I need later, but I rarely see them again at the right moment.

So I built Marginlight: a tiny floating wisdom island for MacBook.

One quote per day. Always near the top. Beautiful, calm, private, and offline.

Not another library. Not another feed. Just one meaningful sentence returning to your day.

### Hero

Marginlight

Your MacBook's daily wisdom island.

Primary button: Download for Mac

### Section: Why

You already save meaningful quotes. They just disappear.

Marginlight brings one back each day in a quiet floating capsule, so your reading life can shape your working day without becoming another inbox.

### Section: How It Works

Choose built-in quotes, select your Markdown quote folder, or mix both.

Marginlight picks one quote per calendar day, remembers it, and keeps it stable until tomorrow.

### Section: Designed For Focus

The island stays compact, expands on hover, and includes tiny controls only when you need them.

Copy, favorite, refresh, hide for today, open settings, or export a square quote card.

### Section: Privacy

Your quotes stay on your Mac.

Marginlight works offline and does not require an account.
